Hi! I'm Sven! I love the beauty and art of film photography, capturing cultural diversity and nature.

In the recent years of my life I am fortunate to being able to travel, live in foreign countries, and immerse in diverse cultures. Traveling became my natural state. Curious? Follow me on Instagram to be part of the journey and get to know where I currently am. Message me to meet up.
