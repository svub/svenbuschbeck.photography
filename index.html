Hi! I'm Sven, a passionate film photographer loving the outdoors, traveling and immersing into new cultures and idea.

This is my portfolio. If enjoy the photos and want to support my work, you can order prints right from this site. My cameras are Pentacon Praktica MTL5 and LTL, using 50mm/f1.8, 100mm/f2.8, 135mm/f2.8, and 200mm/f2.8 prime lenses. I love strong colors, my favorite films are Agfa Precisa CT 100, Kodak Ektar 100.

Traveling is my natural state, follow me on Instagram to get the latest photos and to know where I currently am. I'm happy to meet up!

I love doing exhibitions and collaborations, and am looking for models, please get in touch at me@svenbuschbeck.photography

Apart from photography, I'm doing web technology consulting and development.
